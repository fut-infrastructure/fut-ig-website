Extension: Properties

Official URL: http://ehealth.sundhed.dk/fhir/StructureDefinition/ehealth-devicemetric-properties Version: 3.2.0
Active as of 2024-09-02 Computable Name: ehealth-devicemetric-properties

Properties of this device. Properties are static by nature whereas qualities are dynamic and may change during the life cycle or calibration cycle of a device.
